Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: 2/16/2025

If you wish to have your personal data removed from MeetYourFan, please follow the instructions below.

Manual Data Deletion

  • Email our support team at support@meetyourfan.io with your registered email address and a clear request to delete your account and all associated data.
  • Our support team will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days.

Automated Data Deletion (Callback)

If you are using a platform that supports automated data deletion requests, your request will be handled through our system. In that case, our server will receive a deletion request via our data deletion callback endpoint.

For any questions or further assistance, please contact us at support@meetyourfan.io.
